Hi, I'm Delia Luca, student of UPB.
This web app is the result of my master thesis work. Its goal is to predict a food color image aesthetics.
So, if you have a nice pic of what you've just cooked and you intend to post it on your blog or social media, or fill in for a photography contest, feel free to upload it here and check how beautiful your picture is.

Don't be mad if you don't get a score as high as you expect. Please send a feedback after getting the score and tell us what went wrong, so we can better adapt the model.
